Algebraic Quantum Field Theory

We will focus mainly on topics of AQFT relevant for quantum gravity, such as reconstruction of space-time.


The goal of these notes is to make the incredibly deep and important work of Hans J Borchers more accessible. The goal is to study the consequences of the locality condition and the spectrum condition via Fourier analysis.

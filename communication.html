Communication


Communication can be thought of as a process by which a person updates their understanding of the world via interaction with other agents in the world. In this essay I try to discuss what should be the goal of communication and speculate how this goal can be achieved.



The world consists of a lot of information. A conscious being is something that tries to 'understand' this information.

For the mathematically minded, every person can be thought of as a 'category of understandings', whose objects consist of 'understanding' of the world. Language acts as a mathematical category. Communication then is a pair of functors to and from the category of understanding to the language category. For the physics minded, every brain can be thought of as a state space, whose elements consist of 'understandings' of the world. Communication is a pair of maps from the state space of to and from space of a language. Communication with another person is a rule for updating the state. When we try to communicate with other people we are trying to update our understanding of the world. The goal of education should be to develop a good pair. model I believe every brain acts as a state space, where each state describes their understanding of the world. Communication is a way of updating this information contained in such states. The structure of the state space would vary from person to person, and hence the way they think about the phenomenon in the world would also vary.
